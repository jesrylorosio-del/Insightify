How It Works

Four dashboards. Full picture.

Log in and everything is ready — live data, charts, and exports across four intelligent views.

01
Executive Summary

Total revenue, avg. LTV, churn rate, active sessions, sales trends, payment distribution, and a full transaction log — all in one view.

02
Sales Intelligence

Revenue by product, order status breakdown, sales by day of week, and top customers ranked by total spend.

03
Product Performance

Monthly revenue trends, units sold share, product leaderboard, and avg. revenue per product — sorted and filterable.

04
Reports & Overview

Live activity feed, weekly highlights, revenue snapshots, data health monitoring, and one-click CSV exports.
